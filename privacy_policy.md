# Privacy Policy for Puzzle Maker

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-11

This Privacy Policy describes how **Omnivelox** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application, **Puzzle Maker** (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Puzzle Maker** is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or the images you use within the app to external servers.

### 1.1. Image Data
The App allows you to select images from your device's gallery or take new photos using the camera to create puzzles.
*   **Local Processing:** All image processing is performed locally on your device.
*   **No Uploads:** Your photos are not uploaded to our servers or any third party. They remain on your device at all times.

### 1.2. Application Settings
We use local storage (Shared Preferences) to save your app configurations, such as:
*   **Sound settings** (on/off)
*   **Vibration settings** (on/off)
These preferences are stored only on your device.

## 2. Permissions

To function properly, the App requires the following permissions:

*   **Camera:** Required to allow you to take photos to use as puzzles.
*   **Storage / Photos:** Required to allow you to pick existing images from your gallery.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though this may limit the App's functionality.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses services provided by Google Play Services for distribution and installation. Please refer to [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more information.

## 4. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
